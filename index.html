Moringa School is Africa’s premier tech accelerator, dedicated to transforming driven individuals into world-class software developers and data scientists. With campuses in Nairobi, Kenya, and Kigali, Rwanda, Moringa School combines rigorous curricula, hands-on projects, and industry partnerships to equip students with the skills needed to excel in the global tech ecosystem. Our mission is to bridge the skills gap in Africa by providing accessible, high-quality tech education that empowers individuals to launch successful careers in technology.
